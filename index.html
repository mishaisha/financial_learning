💡 How to Use This Plan

✅ Daily routine: Watch the video (~8-18 min) then read the article (~10-20 min). Total = ~35-45 min.

✅ Click each day to mark it complete — progress is saved in your browser (localStorage).

✅ Day 2 rule: Next morning, spend 5 min writing 3 key concepts from memory. If blank, re-skim.

✅ Flashcards: Make 3-5 Anki cards per day. By week 4 you'll have ~100 cards for review.

📌 After week 4: Paper trade (virtual portfolio) for 2-4 weeks. Read The Intelligent Investor or A Random Walk Down Wall Street next.
